Zhe Li (李哲)

I'm a researcher at Huawei, where I lead a group working on 2D/3D/4D reconstruction, animation and generation using AIGC and graphics techniques. I joined Huawei through the TopMinds Program (华为"天才少年"计划) in 2024. I obtained my Ph.D. from Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Yebin Liu. My research focuses on 3D vision & graphics, especially for humans, animals, scenes, etc.
